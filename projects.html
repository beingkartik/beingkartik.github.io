Markerless Extrinsic LIDAR-Camera Calibration

Ganesh Iyer*, Karnik Ram*, Krishna Murthy, Dr K. Madhava Krishna
Robotics Research Center, IIIT-H [In Progress]

Online Extrinisic Calibration of LIDAR Point Clouds and Camera frames in the context of Self-Driving Vehicles. Alignment using 3D Spatial Transformer Networks by minimizing the Photometric Warp Error. Refinement using Non-linear Pose Optimization.

Recurrent Self-Supervision in Deep Monocular Visual Odometry

Ganesh Iyer, Krishna Murthy, Dr K. Madhava Krishna
Robotics Research Center, IIIT-H [In Progress]

Learning to predict camera pose transformations using Recurrent Deep Convolutional Networks and geometric and Lie Algebraic constraints. We explore the use of Recurrence in 3D Spatial Transformers to learn from multiple views and predict smooth transformation trajectories that minimize the photometric error over the sequence.
